Fire Emblem Three Houses Crests General Information

What is Crest ?

Crests are a special power that only a few people in Fódlan, the main stage of the Fire Emblem Three Houses, have. These are considered as a blessing given by the goddess. Also, those with it can use enhanced powers and spells that give them an edge over others. Long ago, Goddess gave these to heroes and saints, and the descendants of the nobles inherit them.

In Fire Emblem Three Houses, crests are inherited by blood. However, their offspring are not necessarily born with crest, some are born without it. In other words, not all descendants of the owner of crest inherit crest, sometimes it can skip generations. Also, multiple children of the same generation can inherit the same crest.

Crest classified as a major crest that exerts its power and Minor crest that contain weaker. In general, the stronger the hero’s blood runs within the heir, the more likely the heir will own the Major crest. However, in some cases a child from a distant branch of a bloodline is unexpectedly born with Major crest. Therefore, it is not known how Major crest occurs.

In Fire Emblem Three Houses, crests are often considered a sign of fame and respect. Most noble houses cherish this and desperately try to get this as a sign of nobility. Some have been known to marry or adopt individuals of lesser houses or even commoners who have spontaneously inherited a crest just to obtain one should their own heirs by birthright lack a crest. In addition, eldest children who have not inherited crest may struggle with younger siblings in their families. Crest is considered important enough to determine who will be the heir. For example, the throne of the Adrestian Empire succeed to those born with the Crest of Seiros.

Crests in Fire Emblem Three Houses(FE3H)

In the game, crest features combat enhancements that allow your character to use more power in battle. Each crest has its own unique abilities. Also, These are fixed to specific characters and cannot be removed. This is classified as Major crest with tremendous power or Minor crest with weak but potential power.

Just as Byleth has a Flame Crest, some characters have their own crest. However, most characters do not have it. In New Game+, players can spend ‘Renown’ to buy items that bestow specific crests onto various characters. This allows even Crestless characters to obtain a crest.
